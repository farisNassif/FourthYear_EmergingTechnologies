A CNN For Recognising Mouse-Drawn Digits With Keras,OpenCV & MNIST

Draw a digit on the canvas and have the trained neural network try and guess what it is!

The drawn image is encoded, decoded, resized, processed and fed to the trained model. The model then spits out the likely option based on the 60000 images it was previously trained on.

Heres a picture of the structure of the model that the digits are fed to.
